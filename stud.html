My Dearest Students,

Three years ago, you walked through those doors — uncertain, curious, and full of dreams. Today, as I write this letter, my heart is overflowing with pride, love, and a thousand beautiful memories that no photograph could ever fully capture.

You were not just my students. You were my morning reason, my everyday joy, and my greatest teachers. In teaching you, I learned what love truly looks like — it looks like you.

Through every project, every late submission, every laughter in the corridors, every festival you danced through and every struggle you quietly carried — I was watching. I was proud. Always.

You came into my life during one of my hardest chapters. And with your warmth, your silliness, your respect and your love — you didn't just make it bearable, you made it beautiful. You healed something in me I didn't even know needed healing.

As you step into the next chapter of your stories, carry this with you: you are extraordinary. Every single one of you. The world is waiting for what only you can bring to it.

Don't forget where you came from. Don't forget these halls, these friendships, and don't forget the version of yourself that laughed, grew and became right here in BCA 2023–2026.

I will carry you — all of you — in my heart. Forever.

With all my love & prayers,
Ragavi Mam 🌸
